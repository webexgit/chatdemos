Welcome to the Park!

G'day and welcome to Sydney Safari Park, the heart of Australia's wildlife right in the city! Get ready to explore incredible habitats, meet amazing animals, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Our Amazing Animals

Kangaroos

Meet our mob of kangaroos in the Australian Outback exhibit. Watch them hop, graze, and relax in the sun!

Koalas

Look up! Our sleepy koalas spend most of their day napping and munching on eucalyptus leaves in the treetops.

Lions

Hear the mighty roar of the king of the savannah. Our lion pride is a truly majestic sight to behold.
